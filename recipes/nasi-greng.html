Fried Rice

Description

Nasi goreng, or commonly abbreviated as nasgor, is a fried rice dish seasoned with specific spices and usually served with additional ingredients such as egg, chicken, or shrimp.

Ingredients

  • 2 plates of white rice
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok.
  2. Saut√© garlic and red chili until fragrant.
  3. Add egg, scramble until cooked.
  4. Add white rice, mix well.
  5. Pour sweet soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir until all ingredients are well combined and rice is warm.
  6. Serve fried rice with accompaniments as desired.
