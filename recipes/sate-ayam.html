Chicken Satay

Description

Chicken satay is a traditional Indonesian dish consisting of pieces of chicken meat skewered on satay sticks, then grilled and served with a flavorful peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams chicken meat, cut into cubes
  • 10 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper
  • Peanut sauce (see separate recipe)

Steps

  1. Mix sweet soy sauce, cooking oil, salt, and ground pepper in a large bowl.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the seasoning mixture, mix well and let it marinate for 30 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  3. Thread the chicken pieces onto the soaked skewers.
  4. Heat the grill or barbecue, then grill the chicken satay until cooked and browned, about 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Serve chicken satay with peanut sauce and accompaniments like rice cake or white rice.
