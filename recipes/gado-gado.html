Gado-Gado

Description

Gado-gado is an Indonesian salad dish consisting of various boiled vegetables served with a rich peanut sauce dressing.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams potatoes, diced and boiled
  • 100 grams long beans, cut into pieces and boiled
  • 100 grams bean sprouts, briefly boiled
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 100 grams fried tofu, diced
  • 100 grams fried tempeh, diced
  • 100 grams cabbage, thinly sliced and boiled
  • Peanut sauce (see separate recipe)
  • Crackers as garnish
